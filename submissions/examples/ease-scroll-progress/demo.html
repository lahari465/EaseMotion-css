🚀 Introduction

Scroll down to see the progress bar fill up at the top of the page. It shows exactly how much of the page you've read.

The bar fills from 0% to 100% as you scroll from top to bottom.

📖 How It Works

The progress bar calculates your scroll position relative to the total page height. As you scroll, the bar width increases from 0% to 100%.

The label updates in real-time to show your exact progress percentage.

You can easily customise the progress bar style to match your brand. Change colors, height, position, and more.
